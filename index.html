Figure Controls

move -- only on root
a - move left
d - move right
w - move up
s - move down
q - zoom out
e - zoom in

rotate selected joint (and it's children)
j - rotate counter clockwise about y
l - rotate clockwise about y
k - rotate counter clockwise about x
i - rotate clockwise about y
u - rotate counter clockwise about z
o - rotate clockwise about z

to select body part, alt-click
camera controls:
click and drag to rotate
trackpad scroll to zoom
