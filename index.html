Camera Controls

a - move left
d - move right
w - move up
s - move down
q - zoom out
e - zoom in

j - rotate counter clockwise about y
l - rotate clockwise about y
k - rotate counter clockwise about x
i - rotate clockwise about y
u - rotate counter clockwise about z
o - rotate clockwise about z

p - toggle perspective/orthographic
